About Q-Bit

Complex Systems Analysis,
Built for Humans

Q-Bit Corporation develops AI systems that analyze complex human phenomena — from how knowledge spreads through organizations, to how disease emerges in the brain, to how decisions cascade through communities.

Our approach is rooted in mathematical modeling: building AI that does not guess at the world's patterns, but derives them — with rigor, reproducibility, and respect for the true complexity of human systems.

Founded at the intersection of neuroscience, AI research, and complex systems theory, Q-Bit translates a decade of frontier science into tools that organizations can actually use.
